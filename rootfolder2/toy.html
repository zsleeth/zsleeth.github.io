Flip
Frogs

Rubber Band Toy

The design challenge for this product was to create a childrenâ€™s toy using rubber bands and one other material. Flip Frogs are made from plastic and come in two pieces; the legs and the body. The rubber band connects the legs to the body through a simple knot, securing them together and creating the tension needed for the jump. When the back of the toy is depressed, the rubber band stretches and springs back when the toy is released, causing it to spring forward into a series of flips. The challenge is trying to get it to land back on its feet. Flip Frogs come in multiple sizes and colors making them collectible with the added benefit of experimenting with the jumping behaviors of the different sizes. The contour on the frog's back is influenced by the imprint of a thumb, subconsciously suggesting to the user where to place the finger to make the frog jump.
