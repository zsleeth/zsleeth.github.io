Ergonomic Watering Can

An exploration in ergonomics and human centered design, the Ergonomic Watering Can alleviates hand strain with a comfortable handle while maintaing aesthetic interest. The dual spout design allows for air transfer while pouring so the stream of water is consistent and satisfying.

Previous design iterations focused heavily on ergonomics while ignoring aesthetics.
