Hello! I am a 3rd year Product Design student at the University of Oregon and I am the current Vice President of the Industrial Design Society of America student chapter here at UO. I've lived in Chicago and Seattle, but I love the Pacific Northwest and can't see myself living anywhere else! I love trying new things and I am constantly picking up more hobbies, in particular, I love to ski and I just started getting more into animation. In my projects, I strive to reduce my designs to the simplest forms while keeping an element of playfulness. If the product isn't enjoyable to use then why make it?

If you would like to reach me, you can contact me through my email: zakary.sleeth@gmail.com or through my LinkedIn.
